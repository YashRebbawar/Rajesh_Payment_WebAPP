FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I create an MT5 trading account? +
Simply register on PrintFree, choose your account type (Standard, Pro, Raw Spread, or Zero), set your preferred currency, leverage, and platform. Your account will be created instantly and MT5 credentials will be provided by our admin team.
What payment methods are supported for deposits? +
We support UPI and IMPS for instant deposits in INR. For USD accounts, deposits are accepted in INR and converted automatically. Simply upload your payment screenshot after transfer for quick verification.
How long does it take to process withdrawals? +
Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours after admin approval. Funds are transferred directly to your UPI ID or bank account. You'll receive notifications once your withdrawal is approved and completed.
Can I have multiple trading accounts? +
Yes, you can create up to 3 MT5 trading accounts with different configurations. Each account can have different currency, leverage, and account types to suit your trading strategies.
What leverage options are available? +
We offer flexible leverage from 1:1 up to 1:Unlimited depending on your account type and trading experience. Choose the leverage that matches your risk tolerance and trading style.
Is my trading data and funds secure? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level security with 256-bit SSL encryption. All transactions are monitored, and your personal information is protected. Our Dubai-based partnership ensures regulatory compliance and fund safety.
How do I get my MT5 login credentials? +
After creating your account, our admin team will assign your MT5 login ID and server details. You'll receive a notification in your dashboard once your credentials are ready. You can then login to MT5 using these credentials.
